About These Predictions

Performance Equivalencies: Based on competitive middle-distance running data and the established 2.2x-2.3x multiplier for 800m to mile conversions

Formula: Mile time ≈ 800m time (seconds) × 2.2 - This reflects actual performance relationships seen in competitive track

Example: A 1:40 800m runner can typically run approximately 3:40-3:45 for the mile with proper training

Note: 800m is a unique event requiring both speed and endurance. These predictions assume balanced training across all distances.

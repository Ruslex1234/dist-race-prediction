Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculator Chart

Based on CDC Standards - Imperial Units (pounds & inches)

About BMI

Formula: BMI = weight (lb) ÷ height² (inches) × 703

Note: BMI is a screening measure and is not intended to diagnose disease or illness. It should be considered with other factors when assessing health.

Underweight (BMI < 18.5)
Normal Weight (BMI 18.5 - 24.9)
Overweight (BMI 25.0 - 29.9)
